How it works

  1. You submit the form – URLs, platforms and proof you own or represent the content.
  2. We review & triage – group links, pick the best angle for each platform, prepare evidence.
  3. We file notices – DMCA / abuse reports in EN/ES, usually within 24–48h on business days.
  4. We follow up – reminder/escalation after ~72h if the content is still live.
  5. You get a summary – removed, pending and escalated links.
