How it works

Simple process designed for busy creators, studios, and agencies.

  1. Submit the form (EN or ES) with links and proof of ownership.
  2. We verify ownership, collect evidence, and draft platform-compliant notices.
  3. We file the reports and follow up after 72h if the content is still live.

FAQ

Which platforms do you support?
We regularly handle reports on Reddit, X / Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, OnlyFans / Fansly and most major hosting providers. If you have a different platform, include it in the form and we’ll review it.
Do you guarantee content removal?
No one can honestly guarantee removals. We prepare strong, policy-compliant notices with evidence and follow-ups. You only pay the success fee for confirmed removals.
How quickly do you act?
We usually send the first takedown within 24–48 hours after receiving a complete request (forms, proof, and links).
Do you work in Spanish?
Yes — we can draft and submit notices in Spanish for Spanish-language or LATAM platforms. Use the ES form if you prefer to describe the case in Spanish.
